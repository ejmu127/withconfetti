## What Actually Is Confetti?

Confetti is a digital events platform specializing in professionally hosted virtual and hybrid team-building experiences. Founded in 2017 by Lee Rubin and Eyal Hakim, the company set out to solve a problem that's become even more relevant in recent years: how do you create meaningful connections when your team is scattered across different cities, time zones, or even continents?
